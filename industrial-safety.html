Overview

SafeGuard supplies a comprehensive range of industrial safety products designed to protect workers and ensure compliance with occupational health and safety regulations. Our products are sourced from certified manufacturers and meet IS, EN, and OSHA standards.

Whether you need PPE for your workforce, safety signage for your facility, or fall protection systems for height work — we deliver quality products backed by expert guidance.

Key Features

  • ✔ Personal Protective Equipment (helmets, goggles, gloves, ear protection)
  • ✔ Safety harnesses and fall protection systems
  • ✔ Safety signage and floor marking
  • ✔ Gas detection and monitoring equipment
  • ✔ Respiratory protection (masks, SCBA)
  • ✔ Safety footwear and protective clothing
  • ✔ First aid kits and emergency supplies
  • ✔ Road safety and traffic management products

Benefits

  • ✔ Ensure compliance with factories act and OSHA requirements
  • ✔ Certified, high-quality products from reputed manufacturers

Overview

SafeGuard provides end-to-end fire safety solutions designed to detect, alert, and suppress fires before they cause damage. Our systems are engineered for factories, warehouses, commercial buildings, and construction sites — ensuring compliance with Indian fire safety regulations and international standards.

From early-stage fire detection to active suppression, we design, install, and maintain systems that protect lives and assets around the clock.

Key Features

  • ✔ Fire alarm and detection systems (smoke, heat, flame detectors)
  • ✔ Automatic sprinkler systems
  • ✔ Fire suppression systems (gas-based, foam, water mist)
  • ✔ Fire extinguishers (ABC, CO2, DCP, foam)
  • ✔ Fire hydrant and hose reel systems
  • ✔ Emergency lighting and exit signage
  • ✔ Fire-rated doors and compartmentation
  • ✔ Fire safety audits and compliance consulting

Benefits

  • ✔ Early fire detection minimises damage and saves lives
  • ✔ Full regulatory compliance with NBC and local fire department norms
  • ✔ Reduced insurance premiums with certified fire protection systems
  • ✔ 24/7 monitoring and rapid response integration
  • ✔ Annual maintenance contracts for worry-free operation

Overview

SafeGuard's engineering division provides comprehensive MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) services for industrial facilities, commercial buildings, and infrastructure projects. We handle everything from design and planning to installation, testing, and commissioning.

Our team of qualified engineers ensures that all systems are built to code, energy-efficient, and designed for long-term reliability — reducing operating costs and improving facility performance.

  • ✔ Electrical distribution and power systems
  • ✔ HVAC system design, installation, and commissioning
  • ✔ Plumbing and drainage systems
  • ✔ Fire fighting MEP integration
  • ✔ UPS and power backup systems
  • ✔ Building management systems (BMS)
  • ✔ Energy audits and optimisation
  • ✔ Structured cabling and low-current systems
